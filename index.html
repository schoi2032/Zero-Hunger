Our Strategic Pillars

Education

Empowering communities with knowledge about nutrition, sustainable farming techniques, and waste reduction strategies.

Innovation

Supporting technological advancements in cold-chain storage (refrigeration) and drought-resistant crops to secure the future of food.

Policy

Advocating for government regulations that support smallholder farmers and penalize industrial food waste.
